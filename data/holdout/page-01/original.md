# Northwind Hosting — Websites That Stay Up While You Sleep

Every website has a 3 AM. The moment when traffic spikes from a viral post, or a database hiccups, or something entirely unpredictable happens — and the only question that matters is whether anyone was watching. We built Northwind because we were tired of hosting companies where the answer was no.

## Plans without the puzzle

We've watched competitors turn pricing pages into escape rooms, so here's ours in plain conversation. Most personal projects and small business sites thrive on our Breeze plan, which runs $6 per month and includes 25 GB of SSD storage along with unmetered bandwidth. When your project grows teeth — an online store, a membership site, real traffic — Gale at $18 per month triples the storage to 75 GB and adds daily malware scanning plus a staging environment for testing changes safely. Agencies and heavy applications usually land on Tempest, our $45-per-month tier with dedicated resources and up to 10 separate sites under one roof.

Commit to a year on any plan and we knock 15% off. Every plan, even little Breeze, gets free SSL certificates and automatic daily backups retained for 30 days — we consider those human rights of the internet, not upsells.

## The part we're proudest of

Uptime promises are cheap; ours is contractual. We back a 99.9% uptime guarantee with actual service credits, and over the last calendar year we delivered 99.97%. When something does go sideways, our support team — real engineers, not script-readers, working from our offices in Rotterdam — responds to urgent tickets in under 15 minutes, around the clock.

Migrating from another host? We'll move your first five sites for free, usually within 48 hours, with zero downtime in almost every case.

Thirty days, money back, no interrogation. That's the whole risk.
