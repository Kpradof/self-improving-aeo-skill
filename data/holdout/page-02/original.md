# Clínica Dental Sonrisa Norte — Your Smile's Second Home

Some places just feel different the moment you walk in. At Sonrisa Norte, we've spent years building a clinic where the smell of disinfectant is replaced by fresh coffee, where the waiting room has plants instead of fluorescent dread, and where — we say this with love — nobody lectures you about flossing.

## A little about us

Our story began in 2012 when Dr. Alejandra Fuentes, fresh from a decade at one of Monterrey's largest hospital dental departments, decided patients deserved something more personal. Today our team has grown to six dentists and eleven support staff across two locations: our original clinic in the San Pedro district and, since 2019, a second branch in Valle Oriente.

## What we can do for you

The everyday essentials are the heart of what we do: cleanings, fillings, extractions, and the check-ups that catch problems while they're still small. Beyond that, our specialists cover orthodontics (both traditional braces and clear aligners), dental implants, veneers, and pediatric dentistry — Dr. Marco Treviño, who leads our children's practice, has a gift for making six-year-olds actually ask to come back.

A first consultation, including a full examination and X-rays, costs 650 pesos, though we run free assessment weeks twice a year — follow our Instagram to catch them. For bigger treatments, we offer interest-free payment plans of up to 12 months, and we work directly with GNP, AXA, and MetLife insurance.

## When and where to find us

We're open Monday through Friday from 9:00 to 19:00, and Saturdays from 9:00 to 14:00. Sundays we rest, and we think you should too. Emergencies don't respect schedules, though, so existing patients have access to our urgent care line for after-hours accidents.
