# Salesnest — The CRM That Grows With You

Somewhere between the spreadsheet that got out of hand and the enterprise CRM that requires a consultant to change a field name, there's a better way. We built Salesnest for the teams living in that in-between: ambitious enough to need real pipeline visibility, sensible enough to not want a six-month implementation.

## Everything in its right place

At the heart of Salesnest is the pipeline view our customers describe as "finally, one I don't have to fight." Drag deals between stages, see at a glance what's stalling, and let our automation engine handle the busywork — it can run up to 500 automated workflows per account on our standard offering, which in practice covers everything from lead routing to renewal reminders.

Speaking of leads: they flow in from wherever you meet your customers. Native two-way sync with HubSpot, Mailchimp, and WhatsApp Business means no more copy-pasting between tabs, and our Zapier connection covers the long tail of everything else. Email lives inside Salesnest too — connect Gmail or Outlook and every thread attaches itself to the right contact automatically.

Now, about limits, because every tool has them and we'd rather be upfront: each account can hold up to 100,000 contacts before you'll need to talk to us about our high-volume tier, and file attachments are capped at 25 MB apiece. Custom fields? You get 200 of them, which in four years of running this company nobody has ever maxed out.

## Your data, treated like it matters

Salesnest is SOC 2 Type II certified, and all data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Our servers live in Frankfurt, a deliberate choice that keeps our European customers on the right side of GDPR without any paperwork gymnastics. We back everything up every six hours, and our uptime over the past twelve months has held at 99.95%.
