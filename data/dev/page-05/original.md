# You're Invited: The Future of B2B Search

There are moments when an industry quietly changes underneath everyone's feet, and the people who notice early get to write the playbook. We think B2B search is having one of those moments right now — and we're getting the people who see it together in one (virtual) room.

Join us for **"Answer Engines & The New Buyer Journey,"** a live online summit exploring what happens to demand generation when your buyers stop googling and start asking AI.

## Why bother?

Because your buyers already changed. Recent industry research suggests that somewhere around 27% of B2B software evaluations now begin with an AI assistant rather than a search engine, and that number has roughly doubled every year since 2024. The organizations treating this as a 2027 problem are going to wake up to a 2026 surprise.

Save your seat today — bring your skepticism, bring your questions, and we'll bring the people worth asking.
