We know that no two teams are alike. Some are just getting started, sketching their first roadmap on a shared whiteboard. Others are scaling fast, juggling dozens of projects across multiple time zones. Wherever you are on your journey, we've thought carefully about what you'll need.

For small teams dipping their toes into structured project management, our Starter experience brings the essentials together beautifully, and at just $9 per user per month it stays out of your budget's way. Teams that are ready to go further often gravitate toward Growth — for $19 per user per month it layers in the things maturing teams tell us they can't live without, like built-in time tracking and a generous 50 GB of file storage per workspace. And for organizations with more complex needs around security and control, our Enterprise plan is shaped hand-in-hand with your team; pricing there is custom, so a quick conversation with our sales folks is the way to go.

One thing we hear again and again: nobody likes surprises on an invoice. So if you choose to pay annually, we take 20% off, simple as that. And you can try everything free for 14 days — no credit card required, because trust should be earned, not extracted.
