# Brightfield Collective — Marketing that Means Something

There's a moment every founder remembers: the first time a stranger truly *got* what their company was about. At Brightfield Collective, we live for those moments. Since opening our doors back in 2016 in a sunny loft in Austin, Texas, we've been helping brands find their voice and, more importantly, find the people who need to hear it.

## What we actually do (when we're not drinking too much coffee)

People often ask us, "so what exactly does Brightfield do?" And honestly, we love the question, because the answer has grown alongside us. Today our craft spans four disciplines we've honed over the years: paid media that respects people's attention, search engine optimization that outlasts algorithm updates, lifecycle email programs that people actually open, and brand strategy for companies ready to stand for something.

We're intentionally not a full-service agency. You won't find us building websites or shooting Super Bowl ads. We'd rather be exceptional at four things than mediocre at forty.

## The people behind the work

We're a team of 32 strategists, analysts, and writers now, with our Austin headquarters joined by a second studio in Mexico City that we opened in 2021 — a decision that came from our growing roster of clients across Latin America. Our co-founders, Maya Brightfield and Tomás Delgado, still review every strategy that leaves the building.

Over the years we've been fortunate to partner with more than 140 companies, mostly B2B software and healthcare organizations between 50 and 500 employees. That focus is deliberate: it's where our playbooks are sharpest.

## How engagements work

Every partnership starts with what we call a Foundation Sprint — a four-week deep dive into your market, message, and metrics. From there, most clients work with us on six-month retainers that begin at $8,500 per month. It's not the cheapest option out there, and we're at peace with that.

If that sounds like your kind of partnership, drop us a line at hello@brightfield.co. We answer every note within two business days.
